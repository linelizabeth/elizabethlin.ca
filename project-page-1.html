About

Food • Free

Food • Free is an organizaation dedicated to helping food banks get the right resources they need. They believe in educating people to make informed choices so that they may have a bigger impact on their communities. Thus, the objective of this site is to encourage people to donate cash over food as money is the optimal resource.

Link to Prototype

Background

A common problem many food banks face nowadays is the issue of donors preferring to donate food items over cash. Food drives and such are usually full of unwanted items donated by people and not what food banks actually need, leading to food waste.

Strategy

To create a website that focuses on encouraging people to donate money to food banks by using peer-to-peer fundraising and information design so that users will be able to have a larger impact on helping their community food banks.
